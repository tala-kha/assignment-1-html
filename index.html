Simple Omelette Recipe

This is a simple omelette recipe with basic ingredients and steps.

Preparation Time

  • Total: 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional: Cheese, vegetables, meats

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the pan and add butter or oil.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan and cook them.
  4. Add any fillings if you want (cheese, vegetables).
  5. Fold the omelette and serve.
